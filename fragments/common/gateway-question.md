
    __Are the [WAS-110], [X-ONU-SFPP], [HLX-SFPX], or [WT-ONU-STICK] a gateway/router?__

    :   No. These are SFU ONTs (not HGUs) that convert Ethernet to PON; they do not provide Layer 7 gateway functions
        (NAT, DHCP, etc.) A separate gateway/router is required for internet access.
