# Cookie Policy

## 1. Functional Local Storage
The Content is served using a framework (Material for MkDocs) which may use **local browser storage** to retain functional preferences, such as interface theme settings. These settings are stored locally on the User's device and are not transmitted to the website Operator.

## 2. Third-Party Cookies
Third-party services integrated into the site—specifically **Google Analytics** and **GitHub**—may place cookies on a User's device for security, performance monitoring, and traffic analysis. 

## 3. Management
The website Operator **does not provide a centralized cookie management tool**. Users wishing to opt-out or delete cookies must do so through their **individual browser settings** or by utilizing third-party opt-out tools. Continued use of the Content constitutes **acknowledgment** of these third-party implementations.
